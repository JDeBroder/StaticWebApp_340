About Tap Roots Brewery

At Tap Roots Brewery, we strive to produce the best quality beer possible. We started in the Greensboro, NC area and have been pleasing customers and giving people a great place to hang out ever since we opened!

A Feel-Good Experience

We aim to not only make incredible beer, but to give you an amazing experience here. Every visit to Tap Roots Brewery is a chance to unwind and enjoy our finely crafted beers in a welcoming environment. Whether you're gathering with friends, enjoying live music, or simply relaxing after work, we make sure that your time with us is memorable and enjoyable.

Close-Up on Craftsmanship

Our attention to detail sets us apart. From the selection of our locally sourced ingredients to our carefully timed fermentation process, we make sure every step is focused on creating the perfect beer.

The Heart of Our Brewery

Our large vats are where the brewing happens. Each vat is carefully monitored to ensure precise control over the brewing process, from the first mash to the final pour. This is where our passion for quality and innovation meets our commitment to brewing excellence. Every pint you enjoy at Tap Roots Brewery is the result of dedication, creativity, and a deep love for craft beer.
