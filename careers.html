Brewmaster

Job Description: As the Brewmaster at Tap Roots Brewery, you will oversee the entire brewing process from selecting ingredients to the final product. Your role is to maintain quality and innovation while ensuring that our beers meet the highest industry standards.

Responsibilities:

  • Manage the daily operations of the brewing process.
  • Develop and refine beer recipes.
  • Ensure quality control at every step of the brewing process.
  • Collaborate with the marketing team on product launches and promotions.

Requirements:

  • 5+ years of experience as a Brewmaster or in a similar role.
  • Knowledge of brewing techniques, fermentation, and quality control.
  • Strong leadership skills with the ability to manage a team of brewers.
  • Passion for innovation and creativity in beer brewing.
  • Ability to work flexible hours, including weekends and holidays.

If you are an experienced Brewmaster who is passionate about crafting innovative beers, apply today to join our dynamic team!

Bartender

Job Description: We are looking for enthusiastic and experienced bartenders to join our team at Tap Roots Brewery. As a bartender, you will be the face of our brewery, responsible for creating a welcoming and enjoyable atmosphere for our guests while serving our craft beers.

Responsibilities:

  • Greet customers and take drink orders.
  • Serve our crafted beers and provide recommendations based on customer preferences.
  • Maintain a clean and organized bar area.
  • Handle payments and operate the cash register.
  • Promote special events and new beer releases to customers.

Requirements:

  • Previous experience as a bartender in a similar establishment.
  • Knowledge of craft beers and a passion for brewing.
  • Strong communication and customer service skills.
  • Ability to handle a fast-paced environment and work on your feet for extended periods.
  • Must be available to work evenings and weekends.

If you are passionate about craft beer and enjoy interacting with customers, we'd love to have you on our team!
